OutSystems Developer Cloud 2024-04-15

Released on Apr 15, 2024

New

  • Added a new inconsistency to be considered in the impact analysis report for when there’s a discrepancy in the URL of a deployed app during its deployment. (RDEL-2042)
  • Added a new inconsistency to be considered in the impact analysis report for when there’s a discrepancy in the URL of a deployed app during its deployment and there is a screen being referenced in a consumer app. (RDEL-2043)
  • Improved the experience managing Identity Providers on ODC Portal. From now on you are able to update an Identity Provider while it is assigned to the organization or to any stage, without the need to first un-assign it before making any update and re-assign it back after. (RDPP-1560)
  • We have improved the main menu on ODC Portal to make it easier for you to navigate through the Portal features. You can now also collapse those main menu entries that are less relevant to you, keeping you focused on the ones that you use on a regular basis. (RDPP-1585)
  • Improved the experience when managing the assignments of an Identity Provider with a tooltip explaining why certain options may be disabled. (RDPP-1604)

Bug Fixing

  • Fixed the order of the impact analysis report. Now, the report is order by the inconsistency and then alphabetic. (RDEL-2046)
  • Fixed a bug where the loading of the deployment list filters were being hidden after changing the dropdown value to the ‘All assets’ or ‘Any status’ option (RDEL-2131)
  • Fixed an issue when creating a new support case on ODC Portal, where an incorrect validation on the email address format could prevent some valid email addresses from being added to the notification list. (RMKPT-2858)
