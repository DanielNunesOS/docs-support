OutSystems Developer Cloud 2024-03-18

Released on Mar 18, 2024

New

  • For external connections - SAP OData Read integrations are now available in ODC. Use the new connector to integrate with your SAP OData services. For now, only read operations are available. For external connections - Salesforce integrations are now available in ODC. Use the new connector to integrate with your Salesforce data that are part of the Salesforce ecosystem. (RCNT-6964)
  • From now on, when submitting an asset to ODC Forge, you will be able to select which version of your asset you want to be submitted. Previously, you were only able to submit the latest revision. This will give you more control over how and when you share your assets in Forge, without impacting the internal release cycles inside your organization. (RMKPT-2715)
