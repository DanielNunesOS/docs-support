LifeTime Management Console 11.16.4

Released on Feb 20, 2023

Bug Fixing

  • Resolved a problem where the local administrator, also known as the 'break-glass user,' in the cloud infrastructure was unable to clear the built-in user passwords during or after the activation of the OpenID Connect provider. (RDOI-222)
  • Resolved a problem where the local administrator, also known as the 'break-glass user,' in the cloud infrastructure was unable to clear the built-in user passwords during or after the activation of the OpenID Connect provider. (RPM-3582)
  • When an environment is registered in LifeTime, if any other sync process is ongoing there was a chance of concurrent transactions trying to update the permissions of the Platform Services account simultaneously, thus locking the database table for a considerable amount of time. Now, the permissions of the Platform Services account are calculated explicitly during the environment registration before adding to the queue any new sync process. (RPM-3623)
  • Fixed a bug that caused a database exception when cleaning old data from LifeTime processes. The impact of the issue is process log tables growing over time and, if left unsolved for a long period, eventually leading to some performance degradation in asynchronous LifeTime processes executions. (RPM-3682)
